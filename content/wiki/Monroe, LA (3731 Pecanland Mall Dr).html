History

Monroe opened in July 9, 2011 as a Phase 4 store with a Studio C Beta stage. It also featured the 2013 update to the 2005 CEC Remodel Program, which included early Rockstar decor. The location temporaily closed sometime in late 2020 due to the company's bankruptcy. Sometime around 2023, the location received the 2016 cards update, which resulted in the removal of several token games, though getting newer games. The location closed permanently due to underpreformance, likely due to the nearby Tilt Studio that opened in 2019 inside of Pecanland Mall.
